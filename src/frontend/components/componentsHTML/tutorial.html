How to Play

1. Create & Join: A host creates a room, and players join it in the multiplayer browser.

2. Move: Use your mouse to control your sumo wrestler's direction. The wrestler will automatically follow your cursor.

3. Charge & Push: Hold down the space bar to charge power (maximum 2 seconds). Release it to perform a quick forward dash, primarily used to push opponents out of the ring or stop momentum.

4. Points: Earn points by being the last person to touch whoever is pushed out, or by surviving till the end.

5. Win: The goal is to earn the most points and be the last man standing.
